## 🕹️ How to Play Gomoku

1. **Take Turns**: Players alternate placing stones on a 15x15 grid.
2. **Form a Line of Five**: Horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
3. **No Gaps Allowed**: The five must be consecutive — no skips.
